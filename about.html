About Us

Stratosphere Green Energy GmbH, headquartered in Germany, specializes in providing cutting-edge solutions for renewable energy projects. Leveraging the strength of German engineering and the reputation of its technological innovation, the company has established a strong presence in major energy storage markets, including the United States and the Middle East.

Our Mission

We drive the future of sustainable energy by delivering end-to-end energy storage solutions. Through advanced technology integration and operational optimization, we empower renewable energy projects and enhance power distribution efficiency worldwide.

Our Global Reach

We have established joint ventures in Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Kuwait, providing customized operations and maintenance (O&M) services for renewable energy infrastructure. These strategic partnerships allow us to address regional challenges while delivering world-class energy solutions.

Our Expertise

With a global team of 200 experienced engineers, we manage and maintain approximately 5 GWh of energy storage battery assets, ensuring reliability, efficiency, and optimal performance across all operations.
