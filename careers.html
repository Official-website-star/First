Stratosphere Group is transforming the future of energy through cutting-edge storage solutions. Our mission is to build the next-generation energy infrastructure—one that is smarter, more efficient, and fully integrated with renewable power.

Our engineers and analysts work at the intersection of technology and sustainability, optimizing energy systems that power industries, cities, and entire regions. With AI-driven analytics, advanced storage designs, and real-time operational intelligence, we are redefining how energy is stored, traded, and utilized.

We are a team of innovators, problem-solvers, and energy enthusiasts, pushing the limits of what's possible in a world transitioning to clean power. Our solutions already support gigawatt-scale projects across the globe, reducing emissions and enhancing grid stability.
