Product & Services

Stratosphere delivers advanced energy storage solutions, optimizing efficiency, sustainability, and profitability for renewable energy projects.

Business Case & Market Assessment

Conducting in-depth evaluations of energy storage potential, including market analysis to identify key opportunities and strategies for optimal investment decisions.

Energy Storage Design & Configuration

Optimizing storage dispatch logic based on circuit demand, assessing storage capacity for maximum cumulative benefits, and conducting cost-benefit analysis using the proprietary ES-GRID tool.

Testing and Certification

Performing comprehensive performance and safety testing, integration testing, and compliance verification with the latest industry standards.

Power Trading Strategy Development

Crafting and implementing revenue-maximizing strategies for energy storage assets, ensuring profitability through effective participation in power markets.

System-Level Maintenance & Emergency Response

Providing regular O&M services for large-scale energy storage sites, coupled with 24/7 emergency response to ensure quick and effective resolution of site incidents.

Specialized Troubleshooting & Revenue Optimization

Identifying and resolving complex technical issues in energy storage systems while managing income sources such as frequency regulation services and capacity markets to enhance financial returns.
